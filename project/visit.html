Why Plan Ahead?

Zimbabwe's parks each have distinct peak seasons and limited accommodation. Planning early ensures you visit when wildlife is most active and accessible.

  • Secure the best camps and lodges in advance
  • Visit during the dry season for optimal wildlife viewing
  • Match park choice to your preferred activities
  • Allow time for permits (canoe safaris, walking safaris)
  • Check road conditions for self-drive routes
