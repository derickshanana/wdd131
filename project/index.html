About Zimbabwe Wild

A Wildlife Destination Like No Other

Zimbabwe is home to some of Africa's most spectacular wildlife and pristine wilderness. With 11 national parks covering more than 13% of the country, the diversity of habitats — from the Zambezi floodplains to the Eastern Highlands mist forests — is extraordinary.

Whether you dream of watching elephants gather at a waterhole in Hwange, paddling a canoe past hippos on the Zambezi at Mana Pools, or tracking white rhinos on foot in the ancient granite kopjes of Matobo, Zimbabwe delivers unforgettable safari experiences with fewer crowds than many other African destinations.
